Vehicle Description

Prepare to defy expectations and experience the pinnacle of muscle car performance with the 2023 Dodge Challenger SRT Hellcat Jailbreak. Born from a legacy of raw power and relentless innovation, this ultimate American muscle car redefines what it means to push the boundaries of performance on the road and the track.

The 2023 Challenger SRT Hellcat Jailbreak demands attention with its bold and aggressive exterior design. Featuring a menacing front grille, widebody stance, and sculpted hood with dual air extractors, every angle exudes power and performance. The Jailbreak edition adds exclusive touches such as distinctive badging, unique wheels, and a rear spoiler, setting it apart as the apex predator of the muscle car world.

Step inside the cabin of the Challenger SRT Hellcat Jailbreak and experience a world of luxury and technology. Premium materials, including available leather upholstery and carbon fiber accents, create a sophisticated atmosphere, while advanced features such as the Uconnect® infotainment system with an available 8.4-inch touchscreen keep you connected and entertained on every drive.

Under the hood, the 2023 Challenger SRT Hellcat Jailbreak packs a supercharged 6.2-liter HEMI® V8 engine that produces an astonishing 797 horsepower. Mated to a lightning-fast TorqueFlite® 8-speed automatic transmission, this powerhouse accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in just over 3 seconds, delivering adrenaline-pumping performance that will leave you breathless. With track-ready components such as Brembo® brakes and an adaptive suspension system, the Jailbreak edition offers unparalleled handling and control on both the street and the track.

Despite its raw power, safety is never compromised in the 2023 Challenger SRT Hellcat Jailbreak. Advanced safety features such as Blind Spot Monitoring, Forward Collision Warning, and Adaptive Cruise Control help to keep you and your passengers safe and secure, whether you're cruising on the highway or pushing the limits on the track.
